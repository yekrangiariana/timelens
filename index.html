Time Lens

Welcome to Time Lens, where you will be tasked with deducing the year each photo was captured. The game unfolds over multiple rounds, accompanied by a pesky timer to keep you on your toes.

Should you feel the urge to ask for a hint, be forewarned, for it will come at the expense of your hard-earned score.

After each round, you will be presented with a gallery of the photos you encountered. Do indulge in learning more about them if you wish; we have some fascinating stories about each photo.

The game concludes when all photos have been viewed, after which you will be prompted to reset your score, allowing you to restart the game.

Time Lens Rulebook

In Time Lens, players are awarded points based on their accuracy, speed and ability to maintain a streak of correct guesses. The following is a detailed breakdown of the scoring system.

The Correct Guess

If a player manages to guess the correct year, they are granted 1000 points as a starting reward. But, as with any find game that wants to make its rules as complicated as possible so that in appears legit, there are additional factors at play.

The Streak: Should the player be on a streak of correct guesses without using hints, they receive a bonus of 10 points multiplied by their current streak count.

Time Bonus: Proving that haste can indeed be rewarded, for each 5 seconds remaining on the clock, the player is gifted additional points.

The Incorrect Guess

For those unfortunate souls who fall short of the correct answer, there is still hope. Points are awarded based on how close they come to the right year.

A Mere 5 Years Off: In this case, the player receives 500 points, minus 10 points for each year they are off.

A Decade's Difference: For guesses falling within 6 to 10 years of the correct year, the player receives 450 points, minus 15 points for each year beyond the initial 5-year margin.

The 20-Year Range: If a player's guess is within 11 to 20 years of the correct answer, they receive 300 points, minus 10 points for each year beyond the initial 10-year margin.

Wildly Off: Alas, for those who are more than 20 years off, there are no points awarded. Better luck next time!

Quick Answer Bonus:

As a nod to those nimble-minded players, bonus points are awarded for rapid responses:

Lightning Speed: Should a player answer within 30 seconds, they receive an additional 50 points.

Merely Swift: If the player answers within 20 to 30 seconds, they are granted an extra 25 points.

As players navigate this Time Lens, their scores are calculated and displayed accordingly, reflecting the intricacies of this unique system. May fortune favour the bold, the swift and the precise!
